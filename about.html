Currency Fluctuates.

Value does not.

We are an international wealth planning advisory firm created to enhance the financial, intellectual and human capital of our individual, family and nonprofit clients.

We offer financial planning, investment management and family wealth counseling through our Family Enterprise Division, and philanthropic services in an integrated, principle-centered, values-based approach. We built WLI purposefully from the ground up to fill the void that traditional financial planning was missing. We offer considerably more than simply looking at financial statements and estate tax tables and then “running the numbers”. We recognize that there are actually three

distinct aspects to each individual or family’s wealth. Only by fully exploring and incorporating all three aspects of capital – financial, intellectual, and human – can we hope to achieve maximum planning results and maximum benefits to the individual or family. Once our clients complete our Wealth Legacy Optimization Planning Process™, they understand how everything fits together into one big picture; building a master life-plan they understand and find exciting, fulfilling and motivating.
